Kabsa

Kabsa is a traditional Saudi Arabian rice dish that is usually made with chicken, lamb, or beef,and a mix of aromatic spices such as cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves. It's typically served with a side of vegetables and can be made without any nuts or milk.
